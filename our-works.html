Empowering Students Through Vernacular and Enrichment Learning

Kalvi40 brings learning to life for school children through vernacular lessons enriched with songs, and interactive activities. By combining language-based education with creative, hands-on experiences, we make learning engaging, memorable, and meaningful - helping every child explore, express, and grow with confidence.

Kalvi40 Mobile App: Quality Education in Every Pocket

The Kalvi40 App is our step towards bridging the rural-urban education gap. With low-data access, multi-user profiles, story-driven Tamil lessons, and personalized learning paths, it empowers children to learn anytime, anywhere, in ways that adapt to their unique pace and interests.

Key Features

01

Byte-sized Videos

Short 3-minute Tamil videos for all five subjects, using slow-paced, audio-visual storytelling, and key-point prompts to help every learner grasp concepts easily.

02

Practice Tests

Chapter-level self-assessments let students evaluate themselves, review mistakes, and repeat tests independently without a teacher or parent.

03

Timer-based Tests

Timed tests help students practice pacing and build confidence for exams, e.g., 20 questions in 6 minutes.

04

Daily Puzzle

Daily Tamil puzzles (Vidukathai) encourage critical thinking, with answers revealed the next day.

05

Moral Stories

Daily Tamil moral stories strengthen values, featuring classic literature and expert-curated tales, with top story series available anytime.

06

General Topics

A variety of engaging topics broaden knowledge and skills, from French and English learning to reading habits, nature, and Thirukural.

Beyond the Classroom: Other Kalvi40 Initiatives

Kalvi40 believes foundational learning is just the beginning. Our wider programs are designed to open new doors, giving rural Government school students experiences, skills, and confidence that stay with them for life.

Summer Camp

A holistic residential/day-camp experience for Government school students, blending academics, creativity, and life skills.

Robotics Training

Online and offline Robotics training sessions conducted through Google Meet and in-person, making STEM accessible to rural Government school students.

Computers for Government Schools

Kalvi40 has distributed computers to under-resourced rural schools, bridging the digital divide one school at a time. Districts covered: Jawadhu Hills, Kanchipuram, Tiruvallur, Chennai, Chengalpattu & Tiruvannamalai
