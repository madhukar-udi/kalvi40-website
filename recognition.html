Awards & Recognitions

Promising startup award

Recognized as a 'Promising Startup' at the Indian Achievers Awards in March 2021, honouring Kalvi40's early commitment to making language-first, low-data learning available in underserved areas.

Rotary vocational service award

Conferred with the Rotary Vocational Service Award in December 2021, celebrating the dedication of our team to serve rural schools and uplift educational opportunities as a vocational mission.

PMI South Asia Award

Awarded PMI South Asia's 'Project of the Year' for EdTech Kalvi40: Enabling Holistic Education in Rural Schools, recognizing excellence in project execution and impact in rural government school communities across Tamil Nadu and beyond.
